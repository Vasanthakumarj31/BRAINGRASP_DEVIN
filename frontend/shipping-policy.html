Shipping & Delivery Policy

Fast, reliable, and secure shipping for all your educational toys.

Last Updated: May 2026

Overview

At BrainyGrasp Learning Pvt. Ltd., we know you are eager to receive your educational toys. We work diligently to ensure your orders are processed and delivered as quickly and safely as possible.

This policy details our shipping timelines, costs, and our trusted delivery partners.

Our Delivery Partner

We are proud to partner with Delhivery as our primary logistics provider, ensuring nationwide reach and reliable, fast delivery right to your doorstep.

Processing & Delivery Timelines

  • Order Processing: All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays).
  • Delivery Time: Standard delivery generally takes 3-7 business days across India, depending on your pin code.
  • Delays: While we strive to meet our delivery times, unforeseen circumstances or high order volumes may occasionally cause slight delays. We will keep you informed if this occurs.
