Refund & Replacement Policy

Transparent policies built on trust — because every child deserves the best.

Last Updated: May 2026

Overview

At BrainyGrasp Learning Pvt. Ltd., customer satisfaction is central to everything we do. We stand fully behind the quality of our educational toys and want every purchase to be a joyful experience for your child.

This policy outlines the conditions under which replacements and refunds are provided. Please read it carefully before making a purchase.

Replacement Policy

We offer free replacements for products that arrive damaged, defective, or differ from what was ordered. A brand-new unit will be dispatched at no extra charge to the customer.

We do not offer replacements for:

  • Products damaged due to misuse, accidents, or normal wear & tear
  • Change of mind or personal preference after delivery
  • Claims made beyond the 48-hour / 7-day reporting window
  • Products with missing or tampered packaging reported after use

Reporting a Damaged / Defective Product

Customers must report any damage or defect within 48 hours of delivery (or up to 7 days for hidden manufacturing defects). Claims reported after this window will not be eligible for replacement.

Required Proof

  • Clear photographs of the damaged / defective product
  • Unboxing video clearly showing the product condition upon receipt (strongly recommended)
  • Your Order ID and registered email / phone number

How to Raise a Request — Step by Step

  1. Take clear photographs or an unboxing video immediately upon receiving a damaged product.
  2. Contact us via WhatsApp at +91 96774 06373 or email us at brainygrasp@gmail.com within 48 hours of delivery.
  3. Share your Order ID, photographs / video, and a brief description of the issue.
  4. Our team will review your request within 2 business days and confirm the replacement.
  5. The replacement unit will be dispatched within 5–7 business days after approval.

Refund Policy

We do not offer cash refunds for "change of mind" or buyer's remorse. Our default resolution for eligible issues is a product replacement.

A full refund to the original payment source will be processed only in the following exceptional circumstances:

ScenarioResolutionTimeline
Replacement item is out of stock and no equivalent is available Full Refund 5–7 business days
Product not delivered within 30 days of order date Full Refund 5–7 business days
Damaged product (within reporting window, with proof) Replacement First Replacement in 5–7 days
Change of mind / personal preference Not Eligible —
Used or opened products without defect Not Eligible —
Damage due to misuse or negligence Not Eligible —

Refunds are credited to the original payment method (credit/debit card, UPI, net banking, or wallet). For COD orders, refunds are processed as bank transfers. Bank processing may take an additional 2–5 business days.

Order Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled within 12 hours of placement provided the item has not been dispatched. To cancel, contact us immediately via WhatsApp or email.

Once an order has been picked up by our courier partner, cancellation is no longer possible. You may raise a replacement request after delivery if applicable.

Return Shipping

For approved replacements, BrainyGrasp will arrange reverse pickup at no cost to the customer. You do not need to ship the product yourself.

Please ensure the product is securely repacked in its original packaging before the pickup.

Need Help With a Return?

Our support team is available Monday–Saturday, 9 AM – 6 PM IST
