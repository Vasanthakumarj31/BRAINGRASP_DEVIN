5. Payment Security

All payment transactions on BrainyGrasp are processed by Razorpay, a PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant payment gateway. We never store your card number, CVV, expiry date, net banking credentials, or UPI PIN on our servers. Payment data is transmitted directly between your browser and Razorpay's secure servers.

For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, no payment data is collected at the time of order placement.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your personal data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on secured cloud infrastructure. We implement the following security measures:

  • JWT Authentication: All API requests are authenticated using short-lived JSON Web Tokens (7-day expiry). Tokens are stored in your browser's localStorage and transmitted via HTTPS.
  • OTP Security: One-time passwords are stored in encrypted Redis cache with a 10-minute expiry and invalidated immediately after use.
  • Rate Limiting: OTP requests are rate-limited to 5 attempts per IP per 15 minutes to prevent abuse.
  • HTTPS: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL.
  • Access Controls: Admin-level API endpoints require a separate administrator JWT token with role verification.

While we employ industry-standard security measures, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to keep your account credentials confidential.

8. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection law (IT Act 2000 and DPDP Act 2023), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information via your dashboard profile settings
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your account and associated personal data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Opt out of non-essential communications at any time
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Raise a data privacy grievance with our team at brainygrasp@gmail.com

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at brainygrasp@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies & Local Storage

BrainyGrasp does not use tracking or advertising cookies. We use browser localStorage for the following functional purposes only:

  • bg_token � your authentication JWT (session management)
  • bg_user � your basic profile details (name, email) for UI display
  • bg_cart � your shopping cart items for persistent cart experience

These are strictly necessary for the platform to function. You can clear them at any time via your browser settings; however, doing so will log you out and clear your cart.

10. Children's Privacy

BrainyGrasp sells educational toys for children but our platform is intended for use by adults (parents and guardians aged 18+). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 18.

Product browsing data (age-group selections) is used solely to personalise the shopping experience and is not linked to any individual child's identity.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.
