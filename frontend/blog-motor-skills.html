Motor Skills Milestones: What to Expect at Each Age

Published April 23, 2026

Motor development is gradual and highly individual. Focus on daily opportunities: stacking, threading, sorting, balancing, and tracing. These activities improve both gross and fine motor control.

For ages 0-3, prioritize tactile toys and grasp practice. For 3+, introduce cutting, puzzle assembly, and beginner board games to improve control and planning.
